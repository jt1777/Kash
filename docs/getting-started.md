# Getting Started

This guide walks you through everything you need before making your first deposit.

---

## What you need

- **A wallet** — MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet
- **Arbitrum Sepolia network** added to your wallet
- **Testnet ETH** on Arbitrum Sepolia (for KASH-ETH) or **testnet wBTC** (for KASH-BTC)

> KASH is currently running on **Arbitrum Sepolia testnet**. All tokens are test tokens with no real value.

---

## Step 1 — Add Arbitrum Sepolia to your wallet

If your wallet doesn't already have Arbitrum Sepolia, add it manually:

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Network name | Arbitrum Sepolia |
| RPC URL | `https://sepolia-rollup.arbitrum.io/rpc` |
| Chain ID | `421614` |
| Currency symbol | `ETH` |
| Block explorer | `https://sepolia.arbiscan.io` |

Most wallets also let you add it automatically from [Chainlist](https://chainlist.org/chain/421614).

---

## Step 2 — Get testnet ETH

You need Arbitrum Sepolia ETH to pay gas fees and to deposit into KASH-ETH.

**Faucets:**
- [Alchemy Arbitrum Sepolia Faucet](https://www.alchemy.com/faucets/arbitrum-sepolia)
- [QuickNode Arbitrum Sepolia Faucet](https://faucet.quicknode.com/arbitrum/sepolia)

Each faucet gives you a small amount of testnet ETH — enough to cover gas and a test deposit.

---

## Step 3 — Connect your wallet to the app

1. Go to [kash.finance/app](https://kash.finance/app) (or your local dev URL)
2. Click **Connect Wallet** in the top-right corner
3. Select your wallet and approve the connection
4. Make sure your wallet is on the **Arbitrum Sepolia** network — the app will show a warning if you're on the wrong network

---

## Step 4 — Choose your product

Once connected, you'll see two tabs:

- **KASH-ETH** — deposit ETH, earn yield
- **KASH-BTC** — deposit wBTC, earn yield

Select the one you want to use.

---

## You're ready

You can now [make a deposit](depositing.md).
