# Referral Program

**Status:** Planned — not yet live.

KASH will offer a referral program so existing users can invite others and earn a share of protocol fees when those users deposit or redeem.

---

## How it works

1. **Get your code** — Each wallet will have a unique referral code and shareable link in the app.
2. **Share it** — Send your link to someone new to KASH. If they open the app from your link, your code can be applied automatically.
3. **First mint only** — A new user enters a referral code (or uses your link) on their **first deposit**. That binds their wallet to you as their referrer.
4. **Ongoing rewards** — When that wallet later deposits or redeems, you earn a portion of the [protocol fee](fees.md) on those flows.

---

## Rules (planned)

| Topic | Policy |
|-------|--------|
| **Binding** | One referrer per wallet, set on first mint only — it cannot be changed later |
| **Products** | One referrer per wallet across both KASH-ETH and KASH-BTC |
| **Grandfathering** | Wallets that deposited before the program launches will not be able to add a referrer retroactively |
| **Reward rate** | A fixed share of the protocol fee paid on the referred wallet’s mints and redeems — exact percentage to be announced at launch |
| **Payouts** | Accrued rewards will be claimable from the app; payout timing and minimums will be published when the program goes live |

---

## What referrers earn

Referrer rewards come from the **protocol fee** (0.05% on deposits and redemptions), not from the referred user’s principal or yield. The referred user pays the same fees as everyone else; a portion of that fee is shared with their referrer.

For how protocol fees work today, see [Fees](fees.md).
